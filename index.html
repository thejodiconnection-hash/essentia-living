This isn't a perfect transformation story. It's a real one.

450|

451| I didn't lose 215 pounds in a straight line. I lost 120, hit a plateau that lasted a year, and figured out how to break through it. Then my husband almost died from toxic mega colon — and I gained 90 pounds back.

452| I started over. Again.

453| I'm telling you this because I want you to know: getting knocked down is part of it. Getting sidetracked by life — by your family, by fear, by grief — is part of it. What matters is that you pick it back up. I did. And I finished.

454| Through whole food plant-based nutrition, movement, and mindset work, I transformed my health, reversed serious conditions, and at 65 I feel more alive than I did at 45. If I can do it — with all the setbacks, the plateaus, the real-life interruptions — so can you. 455|
